Why Banff

A vacation that feels cinematic in every direction

Banff is perfect for travelers who want dramatic scenery, relaxing town energy, outdoor adventures, and photo-worthy moments all in one trip.

Lake views

Spend your mornings by bright blue water and your evenings watching the mountains turn gold.

Adventure options

Choose between hiking, gondola rides, canoeing, scenic drives, and easy sightseeing stops.

Small-town charm

Banff Avenue is full of cafés, local shops, and cozy places to unwind after a full day outside.

Easy itinerary

Many famous locations are easy to combine into a two- or three-day trip without overplanning.

Featured attractions

Swipe through the must-see stops

This attraction slider is powered by Glide.js and showcases places that make a Banff trip feel complete.

  • Lake Louise

    Known for still water, bright color, and postcard-level mountain scenery.

  • Moraine Lake

    A dramatic valley setting with one of the most famous lake views in the Rockies.

  • Banff Gondola

    Ride up for wide panoramic views and a memorable perspective over the valley.

  • Johnston Canyon

    Perfect for a trail experience that mixes boardwalks, cliffs, and waterfall views.

  • Icefields Parkway

    A scenic drive packed with mountain lookouts, glacier views, and quick photo stops.

Suggested route

  1. Start in Banff town for food, shopping, and easy access to trails.
  2. Ride the Banff Gondola for a panoramic overview.
  3. Drive toward Lake Louise and Moraine Lake for classic views.
  4. Save time for Johnston Canyon if you want a scenic walk.

Plan your getaway

A simple 3-day vacation outline

Use this sample schedule as a starting point, then personalize it for your pace and budget.

Day 1

Arrive & explore town

Check in, walk Banff Avenue, grab a coffee, then catch sunset at Surprise Corner.

Day 2

Lakes & viewpoints

Spend the day around Lake Louise and Moraine Lake, with time for photos and short walks.

Day 3

Adventure morning

Choose a gondola ride or canyon walk before heading back with one last scenic stop.
